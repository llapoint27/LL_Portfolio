Giftastic

Gif Search Application

With Giftastic you are able to click on any of the buttons listed and see a series of gifs populate on the page. When you click on an individual gif, it will animate. You are able to search for any topic and a button will automatically be added to the button section, and you can further click on that newly createdbutton. It's a fun way to search for any gifs you can think of, whether you're favorite ALT band or not!

The technologies used in this app are HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and GIHPY API.

Train Scheduler App
